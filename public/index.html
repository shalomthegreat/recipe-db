A little background before we begin:

  • Your data is completely private since it is not saved to any remote servers.
  • Data is saved locally in your browser or a local MongoDB and persists between sessions.
  • Start your recipe log on your primary device (e.g., your desktop or phone).
  • Each device maintains its own separate database (unless you sync them).
  • Use Export and Load-Data features to transfer between devices.
